Florence Nightingale Data Science Talk Series

Welcome to the Florence Nightingale Data Science Talk Series, proudly organized by the Machine Learning Group at IIT Palakkad. Our talk series is a monthly rendezvous that takes place every last Saturday of the month at 11 AM. We bring together the brightest minds and eminent speakers from diverse domains within the realm of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Deep Learning (DL), and Machine Learning (ML). Each month, we feature distinguished researchers from renowned institutions such as Amazon, various IITs across India, Samsung, Mathlab, and more. These experts will delve into the latest trends, breakthroughs, and innovations in the world of data science, sharing their invaluable insights and knowledge. Stay tuned for our upcoming sessions and mark your calendar for the last Saturday of each month. Don't miss the opportunity to connect with thought leaders, expand your knowledge, and be a part of the data science revolution. Come, be a part of Florence Nightingale Data Science Talk Series, where data meets innovation and knowledge knows no bounds.
